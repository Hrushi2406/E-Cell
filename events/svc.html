UNICUS - the B-Plan Competition of VJTI, aims at manifesting the latent entrepreneurial spirit of young students all over India. The first step to start up big is to have a working plan to stick to. In a way to provide the right platform and support for students who are willing to explore the unbeaten paths of their ideas and believe in leaving trails. With a mission to create a community of job creators rather than jobseekers, UNICUS positions itself as one of the Largest B-Plan competitions in India by helping ideas develop into businesses that have the potential to revolutionise the world.
