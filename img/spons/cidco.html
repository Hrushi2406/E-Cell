CIDCO Recruitment 2017

By Mahesh May 11, 2017 No Comments

CIDCO Recruitment 2017

  • 5 Share this:

CIDCO (City and Industrial Development Corporation of Maharashtra Ltd) are invited application for Walk-In-Interviews for various Class I post from eligible candidates. Candidates can see Interview details, application form and post in below before go to Interview which was scheduled on  19 May 2017.

 

Post 1:- Executive Engineer (Telecom) – 01 Post

  • [ Open – 01 ]
  • Bachelor degree in elect. / Tele communication or Elect. engineer AND 07 years Experience

Post 2:- Assistant Transportation Engineer – 10 Posts

  • [ SC – 02, ST – 01, VJ-A – 01, NT-C – 01, OBC – 02, OPEN – 03]
  • Degree in Civil Engineering with Post Graduate in Traffic & Transportation Planning OR Transportation Engineering or Highway engineering AND Experience Preferable

Post 03:- Deputy Planner – 15 Posts 

  • [ SC – 03, ST – 03, VJ-A – 01, OBC – 04, OPEN – 04]
  • Degree or Equivalent in Architecture or Civil Engineering With Post Graduate degree OR Diploma in Town Planning AND Experience Preferable

Age Criteria :-

  • Age will be below 38 years (05 Years Relaxation for Reserved Category)

Document List:-

  • SSC mark sheet & SSC Board Certificate.
  • HSC mark sheet & Board Certificate
  • Diploma mark sheet & Certificate.
  • Degree mark sheet & Certificate.
  • Post Graduation mark sheet & Certificate.
  • Experience Certificate.
  • Caste Certificate. (If Applicable)
  • Caste Validity Certificate. (If Applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate.
  •  Non-Creamy Layer Certificate. (If Applicable)
  • Leaving Certificate.
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhar Card.

Address To be Sent Hard Copy and Application Form :-

Office of Manager (Personnel) 2nd Floor, CIDCO Bhavan, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai, Pin Code 400614

Interview Date :- 19 May 2016
