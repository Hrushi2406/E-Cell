We Believe in Ideas and Innovation

Ideas

Startup Networking Conference, the annual flagship entrepreneurial summit of VJTI jointly organised by Entrepreneurship Cell VJTI and VJTI Alumni Association, SNC- 2021 is a National Level Event to be held in VJTI Campus, Mumbai.

Innovation

Startups, Mentors, Investors and Corporate companies from across India come together under one roof to transact business, share experiences and showcase innovations. We provide unique mentoring,funding and networking opportunities.

Wallstreet

A one of it's kind virtual stock-market competition where participants have to trade in the stock market based upon the flash news and earn maximum profit oiut of the seed amount they get.

NIC

To promote the spirit of entrepreneurship by helping young startups unlock their true potential by providing them with the guidance and mentorship they deserve.

Consultant

An industrial problem tackling event consisting of problem solving with industrial visit which follows case study structure.

Podcast Series

Panel Discussion

Workshops

Back 2 Business Series

A talk from some great personalities, successful entrepreneurs, CEO’s , big corporate PRs and influencers to inspire students for their life ahead.

Insane Pitchers

It is an exciting event where an idea is proposed to the participants which seems to have very less business sense. The participants are given the task of pitching the idea to a panel of mock venture capitalists and convince them to invest in the crazy idea.

E-Quiz

Entrepreneurship quiz based on different businesses. EQUIZ is a national level business and entrepreneurship quiz competition which focuses on inspiring students and promoting the idea of learning by having fun.
