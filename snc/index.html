Start-up India, Stand-up India

We Believe in Ideas and Innovation

Ideas

Startup Networking Conference, the annual flagship entrepreneurial summit of VJTI jointly organised by Entrepreneurship Cell VJTI and VJTI Alumni Association, SNC- 2019 is a National Level Event to be held in VJTI Campus, Mumbai.

Innovation

Startups, Mentors, Investors and Corporate companies from across India come together under one roof to transact business, share experiences and showcase innovations. We provide unique mentoring,funding and networking opportunities.

SUCCESS. NOTHING LESS.

Enkindling Leadership

Corporate companies from across India come together under one roof to transact business, share experiences and showcase innovations. This Startup Fest is all set to provide unique mentoring, funding and networking opportunities to attendees from all the verticals of the industry.

Learn More
PANEL DISCUSSION

A group of people belonging to a certain industry discuss every aspect of a chosen topic.

WORKSHOP

Workshops about important skills are conducted to equip minds to take on their respective fields effectively.

SEMINAR

Professionals from the corporate fields or popular entrepreneurs are invited to give a talk on certain topics.

PITCHING DESK

The 10 shortlisted startups, will get to pitch for 10 minutes to a panel of investors for an investment of ₹ 10 Lakhs.
