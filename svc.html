Startup VJTI Campaign

Startup VJTI Campaign (SVC) is a dedicated four month accelerator program for student entrepreneurs. It is a mentorship based approach which nurtures both the entrepreneur and the startup from the early stages of idea till the startup is market ready. The competition encourages students to participate in maximum number and be in flow of our guidance. We organize workshops, motivation sessions, pitch practise sessions and demo days. It also helps grooming startups that have literally been born and brought up in E-Cell VJTI.

SVC is a platform for startup ideas at VJTI to get connected with each other and building a perfect networks to help each other grow. Startups coming out from VJTI will get chance to showcase their ideas at annual entrepreneurship summit of VJTI, Startup Netcon.
